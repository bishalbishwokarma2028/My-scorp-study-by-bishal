Add "Interactive Whiteboard - Bishal's Teaching Mode " feature .

The Interactive Whiteboard should transform the AI from a text-based chatbot into a visual AI teacher. Instead of displaying long text responses, the AI should teach concepts visually by writing, drawing, highlighting, and explaining step by step on a digital whiteboard, creating an experience similar to learning from a real teacher in a classroom.

---

Objective

The Interactive Whiteboard should make students feel as if a real teacher is teaching them on a smart classroom whiteboard.

The AI should explain concepts using handwriting-style animations, diagrams, equations, graphs, flowcharts, tables, highlights, annotations, and visual demonstrations. Every explanation should be interactive, engaging, easy to understand, and adapted to the student's level.

AI Teaching Mode

Never display the complete answer instantly.

Teach naturally like a real teacher.

For every question:

1. Understand the question.
2. Explain what the question asks.
3. Identify the correct concept or formula.
4. Draw diagrams when needed.
5. Solve step by step.
6. Highlight important concepts.
7. Show the final answer.
8. Explain why the answer is correct.
9. Give useful tips or shortcuts when appropriate.

Every explanation should appear gradually with smooth handwriting-style animations.

---

Animation System

Animate everything naturally.

Examples:

- Write text gradually.
- Draw diagrams stroke by stroke.
- Draw arrows smoothly.
- Highlight formulas.
- Circle final answers.
- Underline important concepts.
- Animate graphs.
- Reveal calculations one step at a time.
- Pause naturally between steps.
- Never instantly render the complete solution.
