1. Smart Notes – Export to PDF (Desktop)

Currently, in Smart Notes, when I click Export to PDF on the desktop version, it immediately opens the browser's Print dialog instead of downloading the PDF file. Because of this, the PDF is not downloaded automatically.

On mobile devices, the Export to PDF feature works perfectly without any issues.

Please fix the desktop behavior so that clicking Export to PDF generates and downloads the PDF file directly instead of opening the Print dialog.

2. Step-by-Step Solver – Long Question Support

In the Step-by-Step Solver, when the question is very long, it sometimes displays the error:

«"Could not parse Solution – try rephrasing your problem."»

This should never happen.

The solver must be able to handle very long questions without failing and should always generate a complete, accurate, and correct solution.

For Mathematics, Physics, Chemistry, and other numerical problems, the solution should:

- Use simple and easy-to-understand formulas.
- Explain every step clearly.
- Follow standard mathematical and scientific rules.
- Be easy for students of all levels to understand.
- Never skip important calculation steps.
